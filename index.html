Solving any Sliding Tile Puzzle

I once tried to solve a sliding tile puzzle and wanted to know if there was a reliable way to either move a piece where you want without messing up the positions of other pieces, or just a general way to solve them. I only managed to find the second one, but this general technique for solving a sliding tile puzzle does work in such a way that the pieces don't interfere with each other, so my first quest was also completed somewhat.
